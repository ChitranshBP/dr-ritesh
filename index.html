Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-approved, non-invasive treatment that uses gentle magnetic pulses to reactivate brain pathways regulating mood.

Non-Invasive Treatment

No surgery, no anesthesia, and no downtime. You can drive yourself to and from every session.

FDA-Approved & Safe

Proven reliable and safe for treating depression and OCD, with high efficacy rates for resistant cases.

No Systemic Side Effects

Avoids common medication issues like weight gain, drowsiness, or sexual dysfunction.

Quick & Efficacious

How TMS Therapy Works

A straightforward, transparent process designed to get you on the road to recovery as quickly as possible.

Initial Consultation

Discuss your symptoms, medical history, and treatment goals with Dr. Amin to determine if TMS is right for you, or if other psychiatric solutions fit better.

02

Personalized Evaluation

A comprehensive assessment to map out your specific treatment protocol and pinpoint the exact location for stimulation (motor threshold mapping).

TMS Sessions

Brief, 20-30 minute daily sessions over a period of 4-6 weeks while you relax in a comfortable chair. You can return to normal activities immediately.
